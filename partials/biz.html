  • "Keito for Biz" is a team based real time communication and collaboration platform. That will move away people from traditional mail and chat communication paradigm in today’s corporate.
  • Communication and collaboration is important for making decision faster, for less effort knowledge management, for assimilating new ideas by employee connect and for employee engagement. And all this for better working environment and improving employees’ productivity.
  • However today corporate are still depending on communication tools that were designed in 80’s. Predominantly on emails. As per one of the survey people spend on an average 28% of their working time in sorting out mails. And more than 40% people believe that they need less mails. Then there are other collaboration tools, both free and paid, with very poor user-experience or people started moving on social media application like whatsapp for official discussion in informal and unstructured way.
  • Therefor we believe that by integrating user-experience and simplicity of social media into corporate communication and collaboration platform, KEITO, will make people “Be More” to “Do More”
